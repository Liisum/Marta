Marta Kitter

Actress | Singer | Musical Performer

Marta Kitter is a young actress and singer from Estonia with a love for storytelling on and off the stage. She brings energy, emotion and a touch of melody to everything she does.

Contact View Gallery

Bio

Marta Kitter is a young theatre and musical actress with experience in productions across Estonia and Argentina. She has performed in The Sound of Music, Buratino, and El mundo mágico de los sueños as Anna from Frozen.

Trained at the Kaari Sillamaa School of Fine Arts and the School of Musical Theatre in Estonia, Marta has also studied at Hurtwood House and the Guildhall School of Music and Drama in the UK. After a year living in Argentina, she speaks Spanish fluently.

If she’s not at the theatre, Marta is most likely reading literature or singing while cooking — always rehearsing.
